About Sam
+Third year undergrad at UC Berkeley
+Majoring in Industrial Engineering and Operations Research
+Based in Northern California, USA
+Passionate about family&friends, food, traveling
Born and raised in the Bay Area, I have experienced much of what the Bay has to offer. From hikes to coffee shops, vista points to artisan bakeries, I continue to find hidden treasures all over NorCal.
One of my biggest outlets is through photography. I first got into photography when I got the idea that I should document every delicious item I eat (since I am also a major foodie!). From that point on, I continued to hone my photography skills, as well as editing through Adobe Lightroom and various phone apps. Wherever I went, I would always be caught taking candid photos of my friends or the food I ate, which led me to utilize this skill in multiple clubs on campus. Capturing memories is extremely important to me, which is also how I got into film. Creating short videos and montages of vacations are great ways of how I keep memories (vimeo).
